About 關於

Because tuning shouldn't be the hard part 調音不應該是最難的環節

Most tuner apps are made for guitars. Trying to use them on a harp — especially as a beginner or a young player — is genuinely confusing. Harpie is made for the harp and for everyone who plays one: little kids finding their first notes, parents helping with pre-practice tuning, and experienced players who just want something that works. 大多數調音 App 是為吉他設計的。對初學者或小朋友來說，要用它們調豎琴真的很困難。Harpie 是為豎琴而生，也為每一位彈豎琴的人而生：剛開始學習的小朋友、幫孩子調音的家長，以及只想快速調好音上課的老手。

Features 功能

Auto Detection 自動偵測

Just pluck — it knows what to do 撥弦就好，其他交給它

Open the app, pluck a string, and watch it guide you in. No setup, no scrolling through a list — it listens and tells you exactly what to do next. 打開應用程式，撥一根弦，它就會引導你調準。不需要設定，不需要翻找清單 — 它自動聆聽，告訴你下一步該怎麼做。

Reference Mode 參考模式

Tap a string — hear it, then tune to it 輕點一根弦，聽它的音，再調準它

Switch to Reference Mode and tap any string in the list to play a pure harp tone. The gauge stays live so you can tune by ear and by eye at the same time. 切換至參考模式，輕點弦列中的任意一根弦，即可播放純淨的豎琴音。音準儀同步顯示，讓你同時用耳朵和眼睛調音。

Settings 設定

Dial it in exactly the way you need 完全按照你的需求調整

Set A4 anywhere from 430 to 450 Hz, choose flats or sharps, show or hide octave numbers, and pick your lever harp string count. Everything persists between sessions. 將 A4 設定在 430 至 450 Hz 之間，選擇降號或升號，顯示或隱藏八度數字，並選擇槓桿豎琴的弦數。所有設定在每次使用之間都會保留。

Dark Mode 深色模式

Easy on the eyes in any light 任何光線下都舒適好看

Full dark theme — easy on the eyes in low-light rehearsal spaces and pit orchestras. Switch anytime from the settings menu. 完整深色主題，在昏暗的排練室與管弦樂坑中也不刺眼。隨時從設定選單切換。

strings (configurable) 弦（可調整）

FAQ 常見問題

What types of harps does Harpie support? Harpie 支援哪些類型的豎琴？

Harpie supports lever harp (19–40 strings, configurable to match your instrument) and pedal harp (full 47-string layout, C♭1–G♭7, all pedals set to flat). Each layout is built in — no manual setup needed. Harpie 支援槓桿豎琴（19–40 弦，可依樂器調整）與踏板豎琴（完整 47 弦，C♭1–G♭7，所有踏板從降音開始）。每種配置均已內建，無需手動設定。

Is Harpie available on iPhone and Android? Harpie 支援 iPhone 和 Android 嗎？

Harpie is available now on the iOS App Store (iPhone and iPad). Android is in development — sign up above to get notified when it launches. Harpie 現已在 iOS App Store 上架（iPhone 與 iPad）。Android 版本正在開發中，歡迎在上方留下電子郵件，上架時第一時間通知你。

How is Harpie different from a generic chromatic tuner app? Harpie 與一般半音調音 App 有什麼不同？

Generic tuner apps are built for guitars. Harpie has the full string layout for lever and pedal harps built in, a reference mode where you can tap any string to hear its correct pitch, an arc gauge that makes sharp/flat obvious at a glance, and A4 calibration from 430–450 Hz. Every detail is designed around how harpists actually tune. 一般調音 App 是為吉他設計的。Harpie 內建槓桿豎琴與踏板豎琴的完整弦列，參考模式可輕點任意弦播放正確音高，弧形音準儀讓偏高偏低一目了然，A4 校準範圍 430–450 Hz。每個細節都圍繞豎琴手的調音習慣而設計。
